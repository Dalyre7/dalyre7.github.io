The enviromental impact of the internet.

Whether you’re browsing through twitter or sending
emails to colleagues, every action you take online has an indirect impact on the environment.
So I guess you could say that mindless scrolling isn’t only bad for you, but bad for the planet too.
My research shows that many large tech companies have began to opt for more 'green' energy sources to
combat unnecessary carbon emissions. However, the industry is still very reliant on fossil fuels.
