E-waste

E-waste, or electronic waste, has a large impact on the enviroment. A large percentage of discarded electronics contain mined materials that could be recycled/reused.
This contributes to landfill, carbon emissions, and general waste of worthy materials. Depending on the condition of your items, you may be able to sell or donate your old electronics to small tech companies or refurbishing brands. Research shows that residents of Britain alone have an estimated 55, 000, 000 unused mobile phones lying around.


What actually is E-waste?
Electronic products that are thrown away rather than recycled. This can be the older versions of Apple products such as ipods, iphones and macbooks.
All of which may be disfunctional for modern use, run too slow, or be completely broken, yet still contain parts that can be salvaged and reused.


Apple is one of the biggest tech brands there is, so of course, a vast amount of E-waste comes from Apple products.
